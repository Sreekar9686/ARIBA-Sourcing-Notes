4 important files that we need to know when we talk about supplier data from ERP.
pr status.

Draft: The requisition is still Created not yet submitted.
Submitted: Requisition has been submitted with required goods/services for approval.
Composing: PR is still being edited.
Approved: PR is fully approved and ready for further processing..
Denied: Person has declined your requisition.
Ordering: Requisition is in process to generate 1 or more PO.
Ordered: Requisition has been ordered & PO has been sent to supplier via AN.
Receiving: Requester started receiving partial goods they have requested
Received: Requester confirms all orders has been received from their PO.
